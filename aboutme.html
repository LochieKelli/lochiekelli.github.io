About Me

The image above was taken by me, it is of my car. It is one example of my photography. For more visit my instagram: @L0WK8

I live in Queensland with my fiancé and two of my friends from high school. I am a fairly average guy. I spend most of my time chilling out at home, playing games on my computer, and working. I currently work at Shali's Café up in Montville in the Queensland Hinterland area, a pretty awesome place. I have a keen boss who is crazy understanding and is very supportive when it comes to her employees' mental and physical health. Previously I have participated in two subjects.

One was a Dual Diploma of Hospitality Management / Business, which I joined because at the time, it was my dream to run a 50s styled American Diner in Australia. Unfortunately, I have recently figured out that hospitality is not my strong suit. The second was a Certificate III in Photography. I honestly only joined the course because my fiancé and our best friend told me I looked like a photographer, so I should pursue a career in that. Alas, no employment came from this course, and I put down my camera and haven't touched it since.

My dream is to one day open my own online business where I will sell car accessories, and eventually design and create custom car lights. My dream car is a Datsun 260z, has been ever since I was a kid.
